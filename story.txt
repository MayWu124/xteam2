once there is a person named Tony. He does not knowhow to make a story and that's the end of the story.

In order to create an interesting story, Tony asked for help from several people. And he was lucky enough to meet a master in storymaking. The master told him, there are four main elements that Tony has to include in the story.

First, he needed a conflict, which was there from the start. Then he needed a rising action. Finding the story master was a good rising action. Next he needed a climax. He realised that he was writing the climax right now, and all he had left to do was write a resolution. But what should he write...
